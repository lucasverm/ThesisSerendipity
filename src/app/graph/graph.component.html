Welcome to this serendipous routeplanning application. In the search field, you can enter a destination, and additional parameters can be set using the red ball within the triangle. Subsequently, the route will be displayed, indicating the locations marked by red dots, while the gray dots serve as reference points for the red ones. At the right side of the page, a list of the locations to visit and possibly a corresponding image can also be found. Enjoy your exploration.
